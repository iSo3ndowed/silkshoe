about us

The idea for this business was conceived, researched and fine tuned for several months
with a bank account balance that was almost empty. Even with a solid business model,
I was only able to scrimp and save Fifteen Thousand Naira as the startup capital for the
business. The business now transacts in hundreds of thousands and couple of millions.
I, successfully, supplied over Two Million Naira worth of footwears to one of the most
prestigious schools in Osogbo, Osun state. I have sold and delivered more
than five hundred footwears across the South-West of Nigeria. Remember, this journey started 25th May, 2020 because I Just Do It in spite of obstacles.
Now I don't Just Do It , I do it with excellence and together we can do more!

our missions

  • To impact lives through the empowerment of business oriented people; enable these people to start profitable and legal business with or without capital.
  • To ensure people (clients) get appreciable quality for the value of their money.
  • To offer quality products at affordable prices.
